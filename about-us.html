About Us

Dan Gow
Guitarist

Daniel "Dan" Gow (b. December 8, 1995) is an American musician, and the band's guitarist and backing vocalist of the band, Against The Current. Gow was born in Lagrangeville, New York. In 2011, Gow met Chrissy Costanza and Will Ferri through a mutual friend, and the trio formed the band, Against The Current.

Chrissy Costanza
Vocalist

Christina "Chrissy" Costanza (b. August 23, 1995) is an American singer and YouTuber. She is the lead vocalist of the band, Against The Current. Her self-titled YouTube channel, where she posts vlogs and beauty tutorials, has made her a vlogging sensation. As a child, it was her dream to be a musician. She is the vocalist of the band
